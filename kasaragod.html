Popular Places

1.Malla Temple

The mallam durga parameshwari temple is Centuries old. The presiding deity of this temple is Goddess Durga Parameshwari. This temple constructed by the karate brahmins of maharashtra, is well maintained. The special feature of the Mallam Durga Parameshwari Temple is daily Annadanam which consists of distributing food among the devotees.

2.Bekal Fort

Shaped like a giant keyhole, the historic Bekal Fort was built in the 17th century. This historic monument offers a superb view of the Arabian Sea from its tall observation towers, which were occupied by gigantic cannons till afew centuries ago. Near the Fort is an old Mosque that is said to have been built by Tipu Sultan of Mysore.

3.Kottancheri Hills

Kottancheri Hills is a popular trekking destination, and it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. The hills are covered with lush green forests, and there are several streams and waterfalls that add to the natural beauty of the area.
